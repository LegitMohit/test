Ancient ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire with stunning boulder landscapes and intricate temple architecture.

Ladakh

The "Land of High Passes" offering breathtaking landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and adventure activities.

Darjeeling

Famous for tea gardens and the "Toy Train", offering stunning Himalayan views including Mount Kanchenjunga.

📍 West Bengal

📍 Madhya Pradesh

Travel Tips for India

🎫 Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for most destinations. Summer is perfect for hill stations.

🚗 Transportation

India has extensive railway network. Book tickets in advance for comfortable travel.

🍛 Food & Culture

Try local cuisines and respect local customs. Dress modestly at religious places.
